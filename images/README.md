# Where to put your photos

- **her/** — Photos of your wife (e.g. `1.jpg`, `2.jpg`, …)
- **me/**  — Photos of you
- **us/**  — Photos of both of you together

Use any names you like (`1.jpg`, `beach.jpg`, etc.). Then in `index.html`, set each `<img src="...">` to match your filenames (e.g. `images/her/1.jpg`).

Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF.
