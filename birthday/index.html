My Aku,

A few months ago, your name was a name. Now it's the first thought of my morning and the last word of my night. Strange how quickly that happens — and how unbelievably right it feels.

On the 7th of May, you put a ring on my finger and a paper got signed, and for a second it felt like the world held its breath. I didn't. I just looked at you and thought: this is it. This is my person.

7 May, 2026 was — and will always be — the best day of my life.

I don't have a grand thing for your birthday. Just this small corner of the internet, the way I made one for Valentine's — because the only language I know to give you something that lasts is to build it. So this is what I built today. A page of all of us. A page that says I see you on every line.

May 17 always be your kindest day of the year. May this year be the easiest one yet, and let next year find us still standing here together, a little older, a lot more in love.

Yours, on the 17th and every day —
