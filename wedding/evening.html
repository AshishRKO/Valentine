May VII · MMXXVI V of VII

Chapter V · The Night

Strangers in the morning.
Yours by night.

The sun went down on the longest day, and the world felt a little smaller — only as wide as the space between us.

In the quiet that came after —
nothing felt new.
Only true.

And the days kept going — slow, ordinary, ours. A moment, in motion.
